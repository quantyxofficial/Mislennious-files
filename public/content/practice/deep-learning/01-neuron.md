# Scenario

What is the basic computational unit of a Neural Network inspired by biology?

## Options

- `The Pixel.`
- `The Kernel.`
- `The Neuron (Perceptron).`
- `The Tensor.`

## Correct Answer

```
The Neuron (Perceptron).
```

## Explanation

Just like the brain is made of neurons, neural networks are made of artificial neurons that take inputs, multiply weights, add bias, and fire an output.
