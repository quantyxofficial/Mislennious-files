# Scenario

Why are they called "Hidden" layers?

## Options

- `Because the code is encrypted.`
- `Because their inputs and outputs are not directly visible/exposed to the real world.`
- `Because they are optional.`
- `Because they hide the answer.`

## Correct Answer

```
Because their inputs and outputs are not directly visible/exposed to the real world.
```

## Explanation

Hidden layers sit between Input and Output. They perform the "Thinking" or feature extraction.
