# Introduction to Excel

Excel is the industry standard for spreadsheet software, used for data analysis, financial modeling, and business intelligence.

## Key Features

- **Formulas & Functions**: Perform complex calculations.
- **Pivot Tables**: Summarize large datasets.
- **Charts & Graphs**: Visualize data trends.
- **VBA Macros**: Automate repetitive tasks.

## Why Learn Excel?

Even in the age of AI and Python, Excel remains the primary tool for business data. Mastering it is essential for any data role.
