✅ **Meta Tags & Structured Data**
- Enhanced title tags with 50+ keyword variations
- Optimized meta descriptions with CTAs
- JSON-LD schemas:

  - SoftwareApplication (product details)
  - BreadcrumbList (navigation hierarchy)

- `sitemap.xml`: All pages with priority levels
